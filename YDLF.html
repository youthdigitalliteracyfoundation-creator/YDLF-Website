About YDLF

Aim

To foster essential digital literacy skills among students, enabling them to harness technology effectively, safely, and creatively in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Mission

Our mission is to equip students with digital skills, creativity, and confidence to thrive in today’s tech-driven world.

Vision

Empowering every student to confidently navigate the digital world, unlocking their potential as creators, innovators, and lifelong learners.

Credibility & Recognition

Government Recognition

Registered under MeitY

Cybersecurity

This module introduces students to the fundamentals of cybersecurity, including online safety, data protection, password hygiene, cyber threats, and ethical digital behavior.
